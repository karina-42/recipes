Ginger Carrot Soup

Description

A nice, warm and spicy soup perfect for cold nights.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bag carrots (about 5) chopped
  • 1/2 tbsp grated ginger
  • 150g silk tofu
  • 1 tbsp sofrito
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a pot over medium heat, melt butter and cook sofrito and onions until soft.
  2. Add broth, carrots, and ginger. Cover and bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until carrots become tender.
  3. Blend soup in a blender.
  4. Return to pot, add tofu, and stir until hot. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Enjoy!
